# Governance

## Project Stewardship
6X-Protocol Studio is maintained by:
- Matthew C Elliott (`@synryzen`)

Maintainers are responsible for roadmap direction, release quality, and final merge decisions.

## Decision Model
- Contributions are open.
- Product direction is maintainer-led.
- Not all reasonable proposals are a fit for core.
- Maintainers may request revisions, split scope, or decline changes.

## What Usually Gets Merged
- Bug fixes and reliability improvements.
- Tests and observability improvements.
- Docs and packaging quality improvements.
- Integrations and nodes that align with roadmap and quality bar.

## What Requires Extra Review
- Major architecture changes.
- Security/auth changes.
- Licensing/commercial/trust-sensitive behavior.
- UX or branding changes that alter core product identity.

## Core vs Optional
Maintainers may choose to keep certain capabilities out of core and recommend
plugin/module patterns instead when that better preserves product focus.

## Release Authority
Official releases, tags, installers, and Pages content are controlled by maintainers.

## Contribution License
By submitting a contribution, you confirm you have rights to the code and agree it may be
modified, adapted, or declined to maintain roadmap and quality standards.
