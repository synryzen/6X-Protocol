# Code Of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and respectful experience for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, or level of experience.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Focus feedback on code, behavior, and outcomes, not people.
- Welcome questions from beginners and experienced contributors alike.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory behavior.
- Personal attacks, threats, intimidation, or trolling.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Repeatedly derailing issues/PRs after maintainer direction is clear.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may moderate discussions, close threads, remove content, reject contributions,
or ban participants for conduct that violates this policy.

## Reporting
Report conduct concerns privately to:
- `6X-Protocol@gmail.com`

All reports are handled in good faith and with discretion.
